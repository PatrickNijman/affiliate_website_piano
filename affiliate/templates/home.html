Key guide to playing the piano as an adult

Looking to learn to play the piano as an adult but don't know where to start? Keep on reading to find out how others have done it, and how you can too!

Get Started!

Learning to play the piano is a great decision

Before anything we wanted to highlight what a great decision it is you're making!

Health benefits

Playing piano improves functioning and development of the brain! It sharpens fine motor skills, improves dexterity and hand-eye coordination. Music has also been shown to reduce heart and respiratory rates, cardiac complications, and to lower blood pressure and increase immune response. Playing the piano also makes your hands and arm muscles much stronger than the average person.

Social activity

Making music is most fun when playing with other people, you can play with others even as a complete beginner! It is one of the most life enriching parts of playing an instrument, so we heavily encourage you to play with your friends or join a local jam session.

Rewarding

Although learning to play an instrument might seem a big mountain to climb, it can be rewarding every day. By having small milestones along the way, learning to play the piano will be especially rewarding and bring joy to your life!

How to get started quickly

Get started with learning to play the piano in 3 easy steps

Get a piano

Starting with the obvious. You will need to buy an instrument to play the instrument, but isn't it expensive? Doesn't have to be! But there are a few thing you need to keep in mind when buying a piano...

Periferals

You will need some periferal devices in order to play propperly, depending on the kind of piano that you're buying. Getting these as soon as you buy your piano will improve your playing experience!

Choose your learning method!

You may think that with learning to play the piano also comes the added cost of a piano teacher. Not True! There are various book methods and online courses which will help you to develop your piano skill for only a fraction of the price of a private teacher!
